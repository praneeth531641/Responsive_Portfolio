# Portfolio Website Instructions

This document defines **exact instructions** for building and updating your **portfolio website** so it positions you for **₹35–40 LPA Senior / Platform Engineer roles**.

Follow this strictly. Do **not** add extra sections unless specified.

---

## 1. Goal of the Portfolio

Your portfolio must answer **one question within 10 seconds**:

> Can this engineer design, deploy, and operate complex platforms in production?

This is **not** a beginner portfolio.
This is a **senior / platform-level signal**.

---

## 2. Global Rules (Very Important)

- One-page or max two-page website
- Clean, minimal, professional design
- No buzzwords without proof
- Architecture diagrams > screenshots
- Fewer projects = higher senior signal
- Content clarity > animations
